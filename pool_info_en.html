Setup guide

Guide for new users:

  1. Register with your username, password, email and grc reward address
  2. Download and install BOINC client from their site (if you haven't one)
  3. Change BOINC client to advanced view, then use this pool as account manager
  4. If you did that all correctly, you see your host in "your hosts"
  5. Attach projects to your host
  6. Sync your BOINC client with the pool
  7. Wait and sync one more time after 10 minutes
  8. Crunch workunits and wait for rewards (from 2 to 5 days)

If you are changing pools:

  1. Detach from previous pool
  2. Remove all projects from your BOINC client
  3. Attach your BOINC client to this pool
  4. Add your projects
  5. Sync your BOINC client with the pool
  6. Wait and sync one more time after 10 minutes
  7. Crunch workunits and wait for rewards (from 2 to 5 days)

Your RAC on previous pool will fall, and your RAC on this pool will grow (that takes time)

How it works

In gridcoin you receive rewards for BOINC projects when your coins stake. Most people recommend having at least 2000 Gridcoin to stake within 6 months. So, if you haven't got a lot of Gridcoin and aren't planning on putting money into it you can use one of the pools instead.

Each whitelisted project rewarded with equal amount of coins. Rewards distributed between members of Gridcoin team in BOINC in accordance with contribution.

When reward received (when pool's wallet stakes), it distributed between all projects using pool proportion (see Pool stats page). Each project's reward distributed between contributors in accordance with contribution. Then rewards sent.
